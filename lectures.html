Mon, Aug 21. Introduction, fundamental concepts: elements, sampling units, populations, samples, sampling designs.

Wed, Aug 23. Introduction to simple random sampling designs.

Fri, Aug 25. Sampling distributions of \(\bar{y}\) and \(\hat\tau\) under simple random sampling designs. A set of homework problems concerning simple random sampling is now available. You should be able to start on the problems in the section titled “Sampling Distributions” after today’s lecture. Future lectures will cover the other topics in that homework set.
